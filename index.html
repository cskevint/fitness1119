GROUP CLASSES

At Fitness 1119, you don’t see rows of cardio machines like you do at most gyms. We have dedicated ourselves to a functional fitness approach and use more effective metabolic techniques. We use TRX’s, kettlebells, battle ropes and slam balls to keep workouts fun and the calories burning. Our total body group sessions are focused on improving movement quality, enhancing endurance, full-body strength and building skills to achieve power.
